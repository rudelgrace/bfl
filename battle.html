THE FORMAT

Everything you need to know about Battle 3x3 basketball

🏀
3-on-3 Format
Three players per team on a half-court. Fast, physical, relentless. Every possession and every point matters more.
⏱️
14-Second Possession
Each team has 14 seconds to attempt a shot once they gain possession. No time to waste — attack the basket or fire from outside.
🎯
Point Values
Inside the arc: 2 points. Beyond the arc: 3 points. Free throws: 1 point each. Shot selection is everything.
🔢
Game to 50 — Win by 2
First team to reach 50 points wins. You must win by at least 2 — no close game ends on a single basket.
📊
Full Stats Tracking
Every game is tracked in full — 2-pointers, 3-pointers, free throws made and attempted, season totals, career highs and all-time rankings.
🏆
Playoff Bracket
Top teams from the regular season advance to single-elimination playoffs. Regular season and playoff MVPs are crowned each season.

RULES & STRUCTURE

The official Battle 3x3 competition rules

🔢
Game to 50 — Win by 2
First team to reach 50 points wins the game. A team must win by at least 2 points — if the score reaches 49–49, play continues until one team leads by 2.
⏱️
14-Second Possession Clock
Teams must attempt a shot within 14 seconds of gaining possession. Failure to shoot within the clock results in a turnover.
🎯
Scoring
Shots made inside the arc count for 2 points. Shots from outside the arc count for 3 points. Each free throw is worth 1 point.
🏀
The Court
Played on a half-court with one basket. The arc line divides inside (2pt) and outside (3pt) scoring zones.
👥
Team Size
Each team fields 3 active players. Rosters are managed per season and locked before playoffs begin.
👑
MVP Award
Regular season and playoff MVPs are awarded each season based on overall scoring performance and impact.
